Our values are what move us

We act with will, we develop without limits and we love Taekwondo

To our sponsors

We firmly believe that community work is essential for large projects, we are looking for allies on this path who want to strengthen the quality of life through sport, as well as promote the Olympic values: non-discrimination, sustainability, humanism, universality, solidarity and alliance between sport, education and culture what are the foundations of TKD OLYMPIC BOOTCAMP.

Our responsibility with the affiliated brands is to give them projection in an environment where the values of Taekwondo are promoted: courtesy, respect, perseverance, self-control and indomitable spirit, in an optimal space for family coexistence and integration between athletes.

To our community

We are the only ones in the American continent offering an event with these characteristics. Our duty will always be to seek new expectations, bring Taekwondo closer to our entire community and inspire the little generation with the trajectory of great athletes.

We are a strong, united country with great results worldwide; We invite you to be part of this great family.
